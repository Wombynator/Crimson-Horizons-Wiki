Drill

The drill is a specialized mining tool designed to efficiently extract resources from the terrain. It enables players to break down blocks like regolith quickly, making it essential for large-scale excavation and resource gathering in the game.

Drill Image

Obtaining

Crafting

The drill can only be obtained through crafting. It cannot be found or acquired by any other means in the game.

Ingredient Amount
Iron Ingot 4
Gear 2
Battery 1

Example Recipe: Combine 4 Iron Ingots, 2 Gears, and 1 Battery at a crafting station to create a Drill.

Usage

Functionality

The drill is primarily used to break blocks such as Regolith and other terrain materials quickly and efficiently. It allows players to excavate large areas, gather resources, and clear obstacles much faster than with basic tools. The drill is essential for mining operations and large-scale construction projects within the game.

Trivia

  • The drill was one of the first power tools introduced in Crimson Horizons.
  • It can be upgraded with special modules to enhance speed or efficiency.
  • When fully damaged, the drill emits sparks and slows down significantly.
  • Players often nickname the drill "The Digger" in community forums.
