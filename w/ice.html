Ice

Ice is a naturally occurring block found in cold regions and near water sources in the game world. It is a vital resource for water extraction, cooling systems, and various crafting recipes. Players can harvest ice using specialized tools, making it essential for survival and technological advancement in Crimson Horizons.

Ice Image

Obtaining

Natural generation

Ice generates naturally in cold biomes, polar regions, and around lakes or rivers exposed to low temperatures. Players can find large deposits of ice by exploring these areas.

Breaking

Ice can be obtained by mining it with a drill or other suitable tools. Using the correct tool ensures the block drops as an item rather than melting or shattering.

Crafting

Ice cannot be crafted directly, but it is used as an ingredient in several crafting recipes, especially those related to water production and cooling devices.

Usage

Crafting ingredient

Ice is a key ingredient in recipes for water extractors, coolant cells, and other machinery. It can also be used to create pathways or structures in cold environments, and is essential for sustaining life and technology in harsh climates.

Trivia

  • Ice blocks will melt if exposed to high temperatures or direct sunlight in-game.
  • Clear ice is rare and highly valued for its efficiency in water extraction.
  • Players often build insulated storage to prevent ice from melting during transport.
